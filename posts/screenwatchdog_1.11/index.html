Screen Watch Dog v1.11 Released

We just released first public version of our application
Apple macOS App Appstore macOS ScreenWatchDog Release v1.11

This little application will help you to monitor your screen while you are not looking at it. Here is how you can use it:

    1.  Move main Application window to the screen you wish to monitor.
    2.  Press button 'Select watch bounds'.
    3.  Press the and hold left mouse button.
    4.  Draw a rectangle across your area of interest.
    5.  Release left mouse button.
    6.  Application will monitor changes that happened in the selected area.

        If anything changes on the selected area of interest
        application will start playing Alert sound and change
        image border to RED colour.

    7.  You can stop and resume alerts.

        Just press the button with the Bell icon on it.
